#1 How do I Register and Log in to the System?

An instructor is first required to select their user type from the "User Type" form that first appears when they load the Online Web Application. Upon selecting a user type, an instructor will be directed to the registration page where they will be required to enter their details to register with the system.
After a successful registration, an instructor is required to use their Staff id (they registered with) to log in. The system will generate a unique One Time Password (OTP) that they will use as their password and send it to their email.
Therefore, an instructor is required to have internet access during the whole process of registration and login.

#2 How do I create an Assessment?

Upon a successful login, an instructor can access the Create Assessment tab to create an assessment.
The instructor will receive a pop-up modal that will assist them to fill in the examination details. An instructor is then required to click create button to create an assessment. They can then add questions to the assessment and modify the assessment to meet their needs.

#3 How do I Monitor Learners?

Under the "Create Assessment" tab, an instructor will encounter a button labelled "Monitor". To supervise learners, the instructor click the button to join a virtual classroom where they can assess learners taking their assessment.

#4 What type of Questions can I set using the System?

This system offers an instructor to set two types of questions:
1. Multi-choice questions: Questions with options which learners are required to choose the correct options
2. Open-ended questions: Questions that allow learners to express their opinions based on what the question demands

#5 Auto-marking and Submission

Upon completion and submission of an assessment by a learner/ an auto-submission when time alloted expires, the system will automatically mark the multi-choice questions and send results to both the instructor and the learner.

#2 How Take an Assessment?

Points to note
#1. Once the start assessment button is clicked, the assessment begins a count down
#2. Assessments are strictly controlled by time and are auto-submitted when time expires.
#3. Students will be monitored throughout the session and are required to keep camera n audion ON.

#3 Accessing Results
